Dataset:  clinton1.dat

Source: U.S. Census Bureau

Description: County level results for percent voting for Bill Clinton
in 1992 Presidential Election and Demographic variables.

Variables/Columns

County Name  
Votes -     Percent voting for Clinton in 1992   
Age -       Median Age    
Savings -   Mean Savings($)  
Income   -  PerCapita Income($)  
poverty  -  Percent in Poverty     
Veterans -  Percent Veterans    
Female   -  Percent Female           
Density  -  Population Density    98-105      
Crime    -  Crime Index (Per capita)   118-123
